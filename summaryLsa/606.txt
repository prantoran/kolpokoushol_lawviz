(1) If any film club contravenes any of the provisions of this Act or any rule made thereunder, every member or office-bearer of the council, committee or body, by whatever name called, to which, by the constitution of the club, its executive functions and the management of its affairs are entrusted shall, unless he proves that the contravention took place without his knowledge or consent, be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to one thousand taka, or with both.
