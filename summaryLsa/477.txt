WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the Primary Education Act, 1919 (Ben.
Act VII of 1930), and the Rural Primary Education (Supplementary Provisions) Act, 1957 (E.P.
It is hereby enacted as follows:-
This Act may be called the Primary Education Laws (Repeal) Act, 1974.
The Primary Education Act, 1919 (Ben.
Act XXVIII of 1957), are hereby repealed.
