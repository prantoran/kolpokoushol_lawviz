An Ordinance to repeal the Bangladesh Malaria Eradication Board Order, 1972.
WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the Bangladesh Malaria Eradication Board Order, 1972 (P.O.
99 of 1972), and to provide for matters ancillary thereto;
