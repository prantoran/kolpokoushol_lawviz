Any order passed in pursuance of Rules made under section 4 may authorise an officer or officers specified in the order to issue directions for carrying out the purposes of this Act.
Whoever contravenes any Rule made under this Act or an order passed in pursuance thereof shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years, or with fine, or with both.
No prosecution for any offence punishable under this Act shall be instituted except on a reports in writing made by an officer authorised by the Government in this behalf.
No suit, prosecution or other legal proceeding shall lie against any person for anything done or in good faith intended to be done under this Act.
